Two Weeks in Lisbon
Anna Petrova · June 2026
12 photos · 3 videos · 2 audio

Fourteen days with a pocket camera, a tram pass and a voice recorder. This is Lisbon the slow way — hills climbed twice, pastéis eaten warm, and the city's sounds kept exactly as they happened.

DAY 1–3

Alfama & the hills

We dropped the bags and walked uphill until the river appeared between rooftops. Laundry lines, azulejos, and one wrong turn that became the best viewpoint of the trip.

Down to the river

Ferries instead of taxis. The bridge at golden hour looks borrowed from another coast, and nobody on board seemed tired of it.

Cascais & goodbyes
