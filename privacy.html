Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

This policy explains what personal data Albums (albums.ink) collects, why, and what your choices are. The Service is operated by Vladislav Poklontsev ("we", "us"). Contact for anything privacy-related: vgametikok@gmail.com.

1. What we collect

  • Account data. You sign in with Google. We receive your name, email address and profile picture. Your email is used only for authentication and service communication — it is never shown publicly and never shared for marketing.
  • Profile data. The username, display name, bio and avatar you choose. These are visible to other users.
  • Content. The photos, videos, voice notes, albums, posts and comments you create, and the privacy settings you assign to them.
  • Usage data. Basic interaction events needed for the product to work — views, likes, saves, friendships — used for counters and recommendations inside the Service.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) kept by our infrastructure providers for security and abuse prevention.

We do not run third-party advertising or tracking networks, and we do not sell personal data.

2. A note on photo metadata

When you upload photos, we re-encode them, which strips embedded EXIF metadata — including GPS location — before the photo is stored and shown to anyone. Dates of shooting can be read on your device before stripping, solely to offer chapter grouping in your own album.

3. How your content is protected

Media is stored in private storage; it is served only through short-lived signed links generated after the same visibility check that protects the rest of the Service (public / friends / private / per-person exceptions). Your original files' visibility always follows the privacy setting of the album or post they belong to.

4. Who processes data for us

  • Supabase — authentication, database and application hosting;
  • Cloudflare R2 — media file storage;
  • GitHub Pages — serving the website itself;
  • Google — sign-in (OAuth) and web fonts;
  • Payment providers — when you buy paid features, checkout and card processing happen on the payment provider's side, in some cases acting as merchant of record. We receive confirmation of payment and the details needed for your entitlement, never your card number.

These providers process data on servers that may be located outside your country. We share with them only what is necessary to run the Service.

5. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage for your session, language choice and interface preferences. We do not use advertising cookies.

6. Retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. When you delete content, it immediately stops being available in the Service; residual stored copies are purged within a reasonable period. To delete your account and associated data, contact us at vgametikok@gmail.com — we will process the request within 30 days, except data we must retain by law.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Write to vgametikok@gmail.com and we will honour these rights.

8. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. Content that endangers minors is removed and reported to the relevant authorities.

9. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will give notice within the Service or by email before the changes take effect.
